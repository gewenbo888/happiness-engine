# Happiness Engine · 幸福引擎

A civilisation-scale, fully bilingual (EN / 中文) exploration of the nature of happiness, meaning, emotion, suffering, love, flow and consciousness — integrating neuroscience, philosophy, contemplative traditions and AI into one model of human flourishing.

Not a self-help blog. A living atlas with ten interactive systems and one unified model, arguing a single thesis: **happiness is less a sensation than an alignment** — a coherence between self, others, purpose and reality. Pleasure is the weather of a life; meaning is its climate.

## The ten systems

1. **The Origin of Feeling** — valence/arousal emotion map; why anything feels like anything
2. **Pleasure vs Meaning** — the hedonic treadmill vs accumulating meaning
3. **The Neurochemistry Engine** — dopamine, serotonin, oxytocin & the chemistry of mood
4. **Flow & Peak Experience** — the challenge × skill flow channel
5. **Love & Belonging** — concentric circles of connection; the loneliness epidemic
6. **Suffering & Its Uses** — the second arrow, through five contemplative lenses
7. **Happiness Across Civilizations** — Aristotle, Epicurus, Confucius, Laozi, Buddha, Christ, modern psychology
8. **The Digital Happiness Machine** — the variable-reward dopamine loop
9. **Future Flourishing** — AI companions, BCIs, augmentation, post-scarcity, digital minds
10. **The Unified Flourishing Model** — the interactive 8-axis meta-model

Plus a guided **breathing interlude** and **The Flourishing Analyst** — an AI layer that answers real questions through four lenses at once (neuroscience, psychology, philosophy, contemplative practice).

## Meta-model

> Human Flourishing = Emotional Stability + Meaning + Social Connection + Curiosity + Purpose + Freedom + Growth + Consciousness Integration
